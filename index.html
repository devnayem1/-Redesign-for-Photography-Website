The Artist

J. Fletcher

Based between Sydney and the mountains, J. Fletcher has spent two decades quietly documenting the world — its surfaces, silences, and unseen rhythms. The work is not about spectacle; it is about the pause before the moment, the texture of forgotten things, the weight of a glance.

Each print is made on archival Hahnemühle paper with pigment inks that carry a 200-year permanence guarantee. Limited editions are hand-signed and numbered, delivered with a certificate of authenticity.

Shipping & Returns

Shipping

All prints are carefully packaged in acid-free tissue, wrapped in protective film, and placed in a rigid flat-pack mailer or tube depending on size. Free standard shipping is available on all Australian orders.

Australia: Free standard (3–5 business days) · Express available at checkout

New Zealand: Flat rate $15 AUD · 5–10 business days

International: Calculated at checkout · 7–21 business days

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your print. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us within 14 days of delivery and we'll arrange a return or exchange.

Prints must be returned in their original packaging and in the same condition as received. Return shipping is at the buyer's expense unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.

Limited edition prints that have been signed and numbered are non-returnable unless faulty.

Damaged Orders

If your order arrives damaged, please photograph the packaging and print and email us at hello@jfletcherart.com within 48 hours. We will arrange a replacement at no cost.

Customs & Duties

International orders may be subject to import duties and taxes. These are the responsibility of the buyer and are not included in the print price or shipping cost.
